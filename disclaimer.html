Please don't hurt me

I actually think oboes sound beautiful when played correctly, so no need for death threats, stating your close association with the various oboe groups around the world and how you guys are all just dandy. I don't doubt that you are dandy, if a bit odd. I don't doubt that oboe players are great; in fact, I have a lot of respect for anybody who can tolerate playing the oboe and having the patience to do so. They are a different breed of musician, I'll tell you what. Make no mistake, though - while I respect oboe players, I do not respect oboes themselves. They have no redeeming qualities other than sounding nice when played by the right person.
